Outburst

And outburst is a sudden increase in brightness of some astrophysical source. In the context of interacting binaries there are a few types of outbursts:
  • Type I X-ray Burst
  • Type II X-ray Burst
  • Accretion disk Instability
  • Thermonuclear Explosion
